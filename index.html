How & Why We Use SMS

KOZBOTIX

KOZBOTIX uses Twilio to send SMS messages only to users who have explicitly opted in through our website forms, demo bookings, or service activation process.

Common Reasons You May Receive a Message

  • Appointment confirmations for scheduled demos or consultations
  • Support replies to your questions or technical issues
  • AI agent follow-up messages related to your automation setup
  • Important account updates or service notifications
  • System maintenance alerts that may affect your service
  • Onboarding guidance for new KOZBOTIX features
  • Reminders for upcoming calls or meetings you've scheduled
  • Lead automation status updates and performance insights

Your Privacy Matters

We're committed to protecting your privacy and following all messaging regulations. Here's how we keep your information safe:

  • We never sell your data - Your phone number and personal information remain confidential and are never shared with third parties
  • Messages are opt-in only - We exclusively send SMS to users who have explicitly requested to receive communications from us
  • We follow TCPA + 10DLC rules - Full compliance with Telephone Consumer Protection Act and Application-to-Person 10 Digit Long Code regulations
  • Secure Twilio infrastructure - All messages are transmitted through Twilio's enterprise-grade, encrypted messaging platform

Want to stop receiving texts?

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message from us. You'll receive immediate confirmation that you've been removed from our SMS list.
